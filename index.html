FAQ

Everything You Need to Know

IBE® (Invisible Bead Extensions) uses tiny, silicone-lined beads to attach extensions strand by strand — no glue, no heat, no tape. The result is completely invisible, comfortable, and reusable. It's the most requested extension method because it looks natural and protects your natural hair.
A full IBE® installation typically takes 3–4 hours depending on the length, thickness, and desired result. Your first appointment includes a thorough consultation to design your custom look. We'll walk through everything so you know exactly what to expect.
When applied correctly by a certified MASTER stylist, IBE® extensions do not damage your natural hair. The beads are placed away from the scalp and never bonded to the hair shaft. Proper maintenance and timely moves ensure your natural hair stays healthy.
One of the main benefits of dimensional color is its low-maintenance nature. Depending on your hair type and care routine, balayage and root melts can last 3–5 months before needing a refresh. The soft grow-out means no harsh regrowth lines.
Come as you are! Bring photos of hair looks you love, and be ready to discuss your lifestyle, maintenance expectations, and desired outcome. I'll assess your hair type, texture, and goals to create a personalized plan. No prep needed — just bring your vision.
